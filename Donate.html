Frequently Asked Questions

How is my donation used?

100% of your donation goes directly to our programs - education support, vocational training, and community development initiatives across Ghana.

Can I donate in-kind?

Yes! We accept donations of school supplies, equipment, and other materials. Please contact us to discuss your in-kind donation.

Will I receive a receipt?

Yes, we provide official receipts for all donations. Please ensure you provide your contact information when donating.

Can I set up recurring donations?

Absolutely! Contact us to set up monthly or quarterly recurring donations to provide sustained support for our programs.
