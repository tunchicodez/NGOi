Our Foundation

Our Vision

Creating a Ghana where every individual has access to quality education, vocational training, and the support needed to reach their full potential, continuing the inspired legacy of Late Bishop Dr. Augustine Annor Yeboah.

Our Mission

To improve lives by investing in people through education, vocational training, and community support. We are committed to providing sustainable solutions that empower individuals and strengthen communities across Ghana.

Our Programs

Through our diverse range of programs, we're making a lasting impact on communities across Ghana.

iCare Educational Fund

Subsidizing fees for students in formal education from preschool to primary level. Supporting 15+ students yearly with partial sponsorships for vulnerable families.

Learn More

Annor Yeboah Vocational Fund

Supporting apprentices, artisans, mechanics, and specialists in vocational schools. Helping 20+ apprentices yearly acquire practical skills in beauty, mechanics, and trades.

Learn More

Medicare Fund

Providing crucial healthcare support and medical assistance to underserved communities. Medical bills, health screenings, medicine subsidies, and emergency care.

Learn More

The Donations Hub

Bi-annual distribution of clothing, shoes, bags, and toys to communities across Ghana. Over 7 tons distributed in 2024 reaching multiple regions.
